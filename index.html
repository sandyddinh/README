2020 in Rewind

How well do you think you know 2020? Players will try to match their answers with the most popular survey answers.

 

Here are the rules:

  • Each game consists of 3 rounds.
  • Try to guess the most popular answer to collect more points.
  • If your answer is not on the board, your team earns a strike.
  • Once a team reaches 3 stikes, the other team has a chance to steal the points by guessing one correct answer.
  • The team with the most points after 3 rounds wins.
  • Other variations: 1v1, timer
